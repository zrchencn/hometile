Wiki

Mouse

  • left click on the wall to create tile
  • middle click on the tile to select tile (turn red)
  • middle click on the tile again to unselect tile (turn to black and white)
  • right click on the tile to delete tile

Control

General

  • the orbit control is supported i.e left click drag to change camera lootAt; right click drag to move camera; arrow key to move camera

Logic

  • the middle point of the edge selected tiles would be the center of operation
  • the rectangle of edge selected tiles would be the area of selection

Control Panel

  • click 'build furniture' on the tiles tab would add a selected kind of furniture to the scene on the center of operation
  • the 'furniture' tab operation applies to all furniture within the area of selection
